## Share Anonymized Data with Researchers

By enabling this option, you agree to share the following anonymized 
aggregate information with NET Lab, Inc. for research purposes:

**What is shared:**
- Total video opens during school hours
- Estimated total watch time
- Mean elapsed time per video
- Median elapsed time per video
- Rapid browse percentage
- Average video opens per school day
- School days with activity
- Grade level and timezone

**What is NOT shared:** No video titles, no URLs, no channel names, no 
student names, no school names, no district names, no individually 
identifiable information of any kind.

**How it will be used:** This data will be used solely for academic 
research on technology use in K-12 schools. Only aggregate findings 
across all participants will ever be published or reported.

**You can withdraw:** Participation is voluntary. Enabling this toggle 
does not affect your analysis results in any way.

**Who receives it:** NET Lab, Inc. is an independent research 
organization studying EdTech platform accountability, founded by 
Meredith Bouvier, PhD. Data is not shared with any third party.

publicly on the Classroom YouTube Tracker leaderboard:

no URLs, no personally identifiable information of any kind.
